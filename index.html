Learn to make bamboo bikes in Kentuku Burundi workshop


Kentuku Burundi lives in Ghana. He dedicated his life to improving lives of his nabour peasants. He builds bikes that allow people in his village travel faster to their jobs, and their children get to their school which is 5km away. Meet Aggelika Karali, boho jewelry designer and maker

Meet Aggelika Karali, boho jewelry designer and maker


Before she started designing bold, boho jewelry for her brand, Break A Stone, Aggelika Karali spent seven years working as a tax consultant.

“I was brought up in a society that considers working full time at some kind of firm as the main way to be successful. I always wondered if I could just quit my job, ‘break this stone,’ and start working on something based on creativity and hand work.”

Today Aggelika, who is based in Athens, Greece, splits her time between working on her business and raising her eight-year-old daughter.

Angelina Kick
founder, maker
Athens, Greece
